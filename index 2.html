What is in this website?

Hospitable's COVID-19 Guide is formatted into three parts.

The first part shows all local testing centers based on your city location. It provides data on these testing centers such as address, phone number, facility, and hours.

The second part is an FAQ bot which allows you to find out commonly asked information about COVID-19

The third part is a registration page which will redirect to a page where you can register for a COVID Test
